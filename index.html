How We Work

Our Process

Every mechanical and civil project follows a structured, quality-controlled workflow — from initial survey to final inspection and handover.

01
Site Survey

Detailed inspection of site conditions, access, existing infrastructure, and client specifications before mobilisation.

02
Engineering

Drawings review, material selection, WPS/PQR qualification, and method statement preparation aligned to client requirements.

03
Fabrication

Shop or field fabrication of piping, structural steel, and civil formwork — with dimensional and fit-up checks at every stage.

04
Erection & Build

Field erection, installation, grouting, and civil construction with continuous supervision and QA inspection records.

05
Test & Handover

Hydrotest / NDT / load testing, punch list clearance, and complete documentation package handed over to client.
